"I believe that poverty is not something inherent in the poor; it is created by the systems we have in place. The poor don’t need charity—they need opportunity. My goal has always been to provide people, especially the underprivileged, with the chance to improve their own lives, not through handouts, but through access to the tools and resources they need to succeed. I’ve seen firsthand how small loans, or microcredit, can empower people, particularly women, to start businesses and lift themselves out of poverty. This is the core of what I’ve worked for through Grameen Bank and other initiatives—creating a new world where economic opportunities are available to everyone, not just a privileged few."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
